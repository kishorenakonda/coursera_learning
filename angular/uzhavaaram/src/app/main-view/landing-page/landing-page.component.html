about uzhavaaram
"En kadan pani seythu kidappadhe" is the principle of Appar. He was the only sacred saint who indulged in Uzhavaara pani. Vengeeswarar Uzhavaaram is devoted to this principle.
First week : Thiruvasaga Murrothal is being conducted at Vengeeswarar Temple on the first Sunday of every month.
Second week : Uzhavaaram is being undertaken to clean temples in and around Kumbakonam.
Third week : Thirumurai parayanam at the house of Adiyars
Fourth week : Uzhavaaram is being undertaken to clean temples in and around Chennai normally within 100 kilometers of Chennai.
